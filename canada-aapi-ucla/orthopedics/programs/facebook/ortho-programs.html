Orthopedic Oncology

Our orthopedic oncology team offers multispecialty, state-of-the-art care. We have been pioneers in techniques that remove tumors while preserving the limb. Our orthopedic oncology doctors perform the highest volume of surgery for bone and soft tissue tumors on the West Coast. UCLA Health specialists use a wide range of advanced techniques, including custom implants for particularly complex needs, targeted muscle reinnervation for phantom limb pain and expandable implants for limb-length equality.
